Types of boom lifts

Articulated booms for up-and-over access

When you need to reach over obstacles such as pipes, mezzanines or structures, articulated boom lifts provide flexible up-and-over reach with excellent manoeuvrability.

  • Electric and diesel articulated booms from 12m to 26m working height.
  • Ideal for indoor atriums, factories, data centres and congested sites.
  • Zero tail-swing options for tight aisles and sensitive environments.
  • Non-marking tyres and electric power for low-noise indoor work.

Telescopic boom lifts for maximum outreach

For large construction sites, tank farms, power plants and infrastructure projects, telescopic booms deliver long horizontal outreach with excellent stability on rough terrain.

  • Diesel telescopic booms from 20m to 45m working height.
  • 4x4 drive, oscillating axles and rough-terrain tyres for unfinished ground.
  • Large platforms with high capacity for men and materials.
  • Ideal for structural works, cladding, ducting and heavy maintenance.
